The Bozo

Robert Apple always wanted to be a mime for as long as he could remember. Once he was of appropriate mime age he attended Pepperdine mime school (affectionately called Peppermime) and couldn’t wait to get started. After only one day Robert knew something was horribly wrong, he couldn’t understand his mime teachers and no matter how many times he would ask them to speak up, they wouldn’t speak an ounce. Robert quickly became the silent laughing stock of his class and hit rock bottom by dropping out of Peppermime after only one week. Jobless and living in the dumpster BEHIND the Sizzler ($1200 a month), Robert had to resort to a life of mime crime. After being arrested for robbing a silent auction, Robert was thrown into Arkham Asylum where he met the clowns. They taught him the ways of the clown and he swore revenge on all the mimes who did him so wrong. Now that the clowns have been freed from the asylum, Shivers The Clown can’t wait to torment and mock the mimes and the masses of Gotham City. He also can’t wait to boast about his new home, the dumpster behind the Chili’s ($1800 a month)(No salad bar)(Arguably worse).

The Bro

In a different reality we find our old friend Robert Apple back in school, only this time he is not studying in the ways of the mime rather he is studying in the way of Communications. Robert however tends to spend more time at parties than in class until one faithful night he ran into a horrible fate. Mr. Apple may have been slightly intoxicated when he decided to partake in a classic game of "Bobbing for Apples" at a particularly peculiar frat party. Once Robert was submerged in the moderately sized bucket, he found himself unable to come back to the surface, someone was drowning him! Unable to breathe, Robert succumed to the watery depths, the next time he awoke he found himself in a frat party of the dead! Now unwantingly refered to as "Bob Apples", you can find Robert having the time of his afterlife at Scream Break but dont get to close, Bob Apples has grown fond for the taste of blood except if it's O-, since that's like the Coors Light of blood dude, gross.

The Bucco

First Mate Jeremiah Apple was once a revered sailor, known for his unyielding courage and loyalty to his crew. During a fateful voyage through the treacherous Devil's Triangle, his ship encountered a violent storm that tore through the vessel, leaving them stranded in the heart of darkness. Desperate and starving, the crew turned to cannibalism, a sin that damned their souls for eternity. Jeremiah, the last survivor, made a desperate pact with a malevolent spirit to escape death, only to be cursed to roam the seas as a zombie. Bound by the unholy deal, he was condemned to collect the lost souls of sailors who perished in the Triangle, delivering them to the depths of Hell. His once proud visage now a decaying horror, Jeremiah sails eternally, his undead heart heavy with the souls of the damned.
